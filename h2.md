# a)  Vastaamo summary - Darknet Diaries (https://darknetdiaries.com/episode/159/)

## Overview
- Vastaamo was a psychoterapy provider that suffered a major cybersecurity attack in October 2020.
- Theft resulted in leak of over 33000 patients.
- It has been described as one of most damaging cyber attacks in history.

## How it happened
- A guy named ransom_man claimed that he hacked Vastaamo and demanded over 400.000€ in Bitcoins. 
- When the demand was not met, he started to release sensitive records in batches. Attacker made a mistake and uploaded whole database dump.--
- He also uploaded some of his private stuff that helped police in investigation.
- He did not notice this mistake. He started to email every customer of Vastaamo after he did not get any money directly from Vastaamo.
- He got some ransom money of customers.

## Results
- Julius Kivimäki was identied as main suspect because of digital evidence
- Charged with thousands of counts
- Sentenced April 2024 to 6 years 3 months in prison
- Also CEO Ville Tapio was convicted because of failures to protect data

## Home routers
- Router is critical protective barrier for home networks.
- Criminals are interested in home equipments because they are usually the weakest link

## F5 and data breach
- Major security company F5 suffered a data breach
- It is widely used in Finland, everyone should update / upgrade to latest software and check network activity for shady things

  ## Europol and SIMCartel
  - Europol coordinated "SIMCartel" operation that dismantled SIMBox infrastructure that was used for scams and frauds
  - Seizures of SIM Cards, servers, luxury cars, financial assets
 
  ## Expiro virus
  - targeting Windows systems
  - spreads by attaching to legitimate EXE files and steals login details
  - protection requires antivirus, regular updating of system, caution, MFA
